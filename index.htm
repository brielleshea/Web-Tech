About Me

I chose Babson College because I knew I wanted to study business, and I figured that going to an all business school with such good ratings will be useful in the future for my career. I found Babson by actually Googling business schools. Of course, Babson came up right away, but when I saw it I thought it was way too expensive and too much of a good school that I wouldn't even get into it. I eventually applied, and got in and obviously made the choice to come here. I enjoy that Babson is a small school in the suburbs, rather than a city. When I graduate, I hope to have a job in either the fashion world or in business analytics. I had an internship over the summer in fashion, and it was very fun and exciting.
